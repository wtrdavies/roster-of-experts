About the Roster of Experts

Lebanon faces significant climate challenges, requiring locally driven adaptation strategies to protect communities, ecosystems, and key economic sectors. The Lebanon Climate Adaptation Roster of Experts serves as a centralized resource, connecting qualified professionals with organizations, policymakers, and project teams working on climate resilience.

By leveraging national expertise in areas such as water management, agriculture, renewable energy, and environmental policy, the roster facilitates rapid mobilization of specialists, enhances capacity-building efforts, and strengthens local ownership of adaptation initiatives.

This initiative ensures that adaptation projects in Lebanon benefit from local knowledge, innovative solutions, and targeted technical expertise, driving effective and sustainable climate action.
